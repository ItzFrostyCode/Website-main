ABOUT ME

Hello there! I'm Joshua Wayman A. Arabejo, a dedicated student and aspiring software developer residing in the vibrant city of Davao, Philippines. With a passion for all things tech, I specialize in web development, UI/UX design, and desktop applications, weaving creativity and functionality into the digital landscape.

Coding is not just a subject for me; it's a way of exploration and growth. I thrive on turning ideas into reality, bringing visions to life through lines of code and thoughtful design. From front-end aesthetics using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to back-end development with C# and database management with MySQL, I am building my expertise across the entire spectrum of development.

My journey in the world of software development is a story of continuous learning and innovation. Each project is an opportunity to explore new horizons, embrace challenges, and refine my skills. Whether it's crafting intuitive user interfaces on Figma, building responsive websites, or developing desktop applications with Windows Forms, I take pride in delivering solutions that not only meet but exceed expectations.

Currently pursuing my degree while actively building my portfolio, I believe in learning through practice and real-world application. My goal is to transition from a passionate student to a professional developer who creates meaningful digital experiences.
